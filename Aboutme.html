I love expressing my ideas in words. You never know how your learnings can inspire someone else. Here's where I pen down my experiences, introspect about current issues and elaborate on interesting research talks. I've been a writer at Coinmonks, IECSE and ACM-W Manipal.

Fast and Scalable Estimation of Uncertainty using Bayesian Deep Learning [Link]
Dr. Emtiyaz Khan's insights on fast computation of uncertainity and Variational ADAM. He walked us through variational inference, Bayesian models, natural gradients and fast Gaussian approximation for deep learning models.

Learn How to Train U-Net On Your Dataset [Link]
A step-by-step guide on how to train U-Net (since I found the original documentation too abstract). Several comments and issues on Github helped me in figuring out the training steps.

Graph Convolutional Neural Networks- A Talk at IISc [Link]
Dr. Tijmen Tieleman introduced us to the bleeding edge research in graph convolutional neural networks.

An IISc Seminar: The Beginning of VNIR [Link]
A startup centric talk conducted by Dr. Meher Prakash. He sheds light on his journey from CalTech and ETH Zurich to founding VNIR Biotechnologies Pvt Ltd.

An IISc Lecture: Deep Learning Research- Representation Learning [Link]
Prof. Sargur Narasimhamurthy Srihari touched upon various topics including the significance of representation, representation learning methods, transfer learning, disentangling variables and explainable AI.
